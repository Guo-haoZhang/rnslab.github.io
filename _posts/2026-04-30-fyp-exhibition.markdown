In June 2025, Jiayi Zhou (Louise), Qing Lin Tan (Giselle), Sahat Mahmud, and Sashenka Gamage establish a team for the Inter-departmental final year project (funded by FENG), to conduct the research topic of "Intelligent UAV Systems for GNSS-Based Remote Sensing on Vegetation". Through nine months of explorations, they achieve fruitful results on using the UAV to capture GNSS observations from ground reflections to detect ground features like water, vegetation, etc.
