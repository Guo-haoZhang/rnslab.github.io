Our PhD student Yilin presents her research at the European Navigation Conference 2026

Apr 28, 2026

Ms Yilin Fan attends the European Navigation Conference (ENC) 2026 in Vienna, Austria, and presents her research entitled “Enhancing Urban GNSS Measurement Accuracy: A Statistical 3D Mapping Aided NLOS Error Correction Framework”. This study extends 3D mapping aided (3DMA) GNSS by a statistical process to estimate the corrections for non-line-of-sight reception errors in urban areas.
