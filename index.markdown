    <em style="font-size: 1.1em;">Welcome to the Radio Navigation and Sensing (RNS) Laboratory of the Department of Aeronautical and Aviation Engineering at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University. Our research focuses on using radio signals (e.g., GNSS, LEO satellite signals, Wi-Fi, etc.) to achieve positioning, navigation, timing (PNT), as well as sensing in challenging environments. We develop innovative algorithms and systems leveraging 3D mapping-aided GNSS, radiolocation, signal propagation modeling, and deep learning to advance reliable localization for civil applications in urban and indoor areas. We welcome collaborations and are committed to pushing the frontiers of navigation and sensing science.</em>
