We are seeking highly motivated, talented, and research-oriented candidates to join our research team. Interested applicants are welcome to contact the laboratory’s principal investigator, Dr. Guohao Zhang (Email: gh.zhang@polyu.edu.hk), with your CV.

<br>

### Postgraduate Students for 2027-Fall Admission

Passion & Foundation: Enthusiastic about research and study, strong team collaboration spirit, proactive, with a solid foundation in mathematics and physics. Proficient in programming and data processing skills, with a certain level of background in navigation/positioning, signal propagation, or estimation algorithms.

English Language Proficiency: Meet the university standard English test score requirements: IELTS (≥ 6.5) or TOEFL (≥ 80).

Prior research experiences in GNSS signal processing, signal propagation modeling, remote sensing, or deep learning assisted GNSS will be given priority for consideration. Outstanding applicants can be supported and nominated for the Hong Kong PhD Fellowship Scheme application.

<br>

### Postdoctoral Fellow

Academic Record: A strong track record of academic excellence, evidenced by first-author publications in top-tier journals.

Research Expertise: Extensive research experience in GNSS and low Earth orbit satellite positioning, navigation, and timing (LEO PNT) or GNSS remote sensing.
